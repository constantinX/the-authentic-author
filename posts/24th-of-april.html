3:00 pm

My action drive her even further away. What is the one reason I could give her that allows her to stay? I'm the villain, she thinks. But I am not. I don't want to be. I cannot be.

Why wasn't I able to give her love? As much love as she deserved? Why did I feel like I didn't get the love and understanding that I deserved? As much as I have been wrong, she couldn't understand me in many ways. I didn't think she truly wanted to. Maybe I was wrong.
