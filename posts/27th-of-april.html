6:48 am

I feel weird. Good morning, man of the future. I came to a realization. I have to work on the man I want to be and I have to hope that she chooses this man to be with. For the kids, for the family, for us. I don't yet know how I can do that while feeling torn in many ways. On the one hand I notice that there is something in my past that need to be addressed, that holds me back. On the other hand, I have to look to the future. I have to be the best self for me, my kids and my wife.

But who is the man that I want to be. I am not sure. If I close my eyes, if I try to imagine that person, I see a man that is successful with his own business, a well dressed man who is charming and funny, a man that sits down at a piano and plays wonderfully for his love interest. An author that is sensitive to the world around him in ways others cannot. I see a caring man who takes care of his children and his family, I see an interesting man who captivates the table, I see crying man who allows himself to express what he feels.

I need to think deeply about that vision. Is that what I really want? Is that vision complete, is that vision too shallow? Am I running after wannabe images from Hollywood?

What can I do now?

I think the easiest is to simply choose a few things from that future-self-list and start working on it, start showing results. I will tackle the ones first that yield results faster, those shall be my priority.

  • #1 "a well dressed man who is charming and funny"
  • #2 "a caring man who takes care of his children and his family"
  • #3 "an interesting man who captivates the table"

Maybe some of these things I am already or at least partially already. But I think I can do better.

Goal 1

A well dressed man who is charming and funny. I will change my style. I have been too sloppy in the past. I think I need another shirt, another sacco, some new pants and another set of shoes. I will get myself some accessories. A necklace, a wristband and a ring. I used to have that. I used to wear that. I forgot about it. I will find trainings on how to be charming. I will read those training resources, I will practice these training resources every evening in front of the mirror. I need to figure out why she doesn't laugh at my current jokes. How does our humour differ? I will research techniques to be more approachable and funny.

Goal 2

A caring man who takes care of his children and his family. Be humble, listen, be kind. Smile at our children, teach them integrity and values. Encourage them to cry if they need to. I will make an deliberate effort to listen more carefully. I will make an effort to be kinder.

6:05 pm

My feelings go crazy again. I have a hard time. I want to go back. I want to resolve our problems, ok, it takes time, but I want to win her back. Right now, she doesn't have an income. Any income generated would likely have to go towards her studies. That wouldn't really allow her to move out. I won't accept a divorce. I will not leave. I will work, I am in it for the long game. This drags me down tremendously. I came clear with her today. Told her some specifics about my history of abuse, told her about my pornography addiction. She was a little surprised. But I think I made clear that I am not speaking from a point of weakness, but really from a point of strength, openness and reflection. It had to be said.

6:09 pm

I want to build my business now. I will put together an authority page and build an email list.
