10:37 am

My heart hurts. I had quite some physical pain today. My feelings must have manifested themselves. Windy, blue sky, beautiful day. I got an air fryer and a "Bollerwagen". Lua wanted one. She has been asking for that for some time already. I am happy that I did that.

I have to turn around. Do the opposite of what she expects me to do. She expected me to be negative, suffocating her, calling and texting her all the time. Be all over her. I want to do the opposite. I won't text her, won't call her. This will be tough. I will only greet her with a smile. I will ask short questions and answer in close ended answers. I will show her that I have my own social circle, make it a mystery to her. I have to build a social circle and demonstrate that I am not the grumpy guy in the basement.

What do I really like? I like coding. I like to be in shape. Can I find a coding thing for me here? Can I find a reasonbly expensive gym here?

9:42 pm - Reiki

I laid down onto the bed, closed my eyes and starting breathing slowly and deeply. I felt how my body began to tickle ever so slightly. She sat down next to me. Closed her eyes too. Touch my chest, laid her hands onto my lower stomach. I felt her warm hands. She did not know that I had pain in the stomach, but she recognized it too. Breathing, wandering mind. I started feeling more intense pain under her hands. Like hot needles it poked my skin, penetrated my flesh. My stomach began to rumble, working and moving. My chest felt heat and cold. I am not sure if she took her hands away or if she kept them at all times on me. My sensations culminated in several strong, walloping waves releasing themselves beneath her hands throughout my body.

My pain withered away, it was replaced by intense emotions, unprovoked, erupting within me. A strong feeling of regret overcame me. Regret for having closed myself for many years to the experience I had tonight. Regret that I allowed myself to have a close mind again, regret for allowing myself to become blinded by arguments, blinded by conviction, blinded by arrogance. I cried. Tears just rolled down the side of my face. My eyes, still closed, my face, still motionless, I felt the wet teardrops making their way down my cheeks, rolling over my neck.

She slowly took her hands off of me. It acted almost like a force multiplier. My emotions burst out. I felt shame. Shame for who I am, shame for who I was. I felt shame for what I have done to her. Shame for not being there for her. It was my blindness, my preoccupation with everything else. I am ashamed. My beautiful girl went through this tremendous amount of pain and I was too busy, to blind, to lazy to see and to recognize. Too broken to act, too tired to do something meaningful for her. I was dismissive, I was distant. I did not allow myself to see her the way she was and wanted to be. I have done very wrong.

I acknowledge my feelings.
