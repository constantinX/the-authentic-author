Tuesday, 6:27 am

Yesterday was a weird day. I feel lonely. I feel like ending everything. I feel like my heart is clenched and hurting. I feel like my work is not seen, like I am not loved. I did wrong. I was a poor husband. I understand that now. I acknowledge that. I feel like I want to be held by her and I want to hold her. I want to show her how sorry I am. But I am not allowed. I want to end it. I want to die. If there weren't the kids. I want them to have a family, a family that works through things and comes out stronger. Why doesn't she love me anymore? Was I so bad? I was a poor husband. I was a very poor husband. I was not able to fulfill my wifes needs. I was not able to fulfill my wifes needs. I was only able to care for financials and work and that stuff. I wasn't able to give her more. I feel so bad about it. I feel so bad.

I cannot survive this much longer. I need to get into the phase of rebuilding myself to show her what I am really made of. Crying does not help. I feel like I want her to hold me, but that is the very thing that pushes her away. I need to give her space. I need to stop being needy. She smells that. I need to be able to impress her. I need to be able to show her my changes without forcing the situation. I need to be able to win her back. How can I do that?

I acknowledged her wish for a divorce yesterday and talked to her about the changes that I am making. For a moment it felt like I am coming through. For a moment it felt I touched her. But I don't know if it was a good touch. I am so unsure of the future. And the two kids are in Canada, in the middle of that. I cannot believe that. I feel like my heart gets ripped out of my chest. I feel like dying. I am so sad. I am so so sad. I want this to end. Why can I not be stronger? Why can I not be more compelling? Why can I not be the better man? What does it take for her to see me? What does it take for her to come back?

I feel empty. I feel like a shell. I feel like a dead man walking. I am broken and left alone to die. I am not able to be so strong. I am unable to carry the load of everything and be the one that saves our relationship.

I get so frustrated and angry when I see this dude on her Instagram account. When I see him liking her videos. I get furios. I cannot control it. I cannot control it. I want to die. Be done with it. I want this asshole dead, out of my life, away from my wife. Why am I not allowed to fix it?

I need to float couples therapy again. She promised me to go there. I need to build trust back, I need to build safety back. I need to give her the ability to talk to me. I need to allow her to be herself, give her space. I need to work on my communication. I need to become emotionally bulletproof, I need to shift my identity, change who I am. How can I do all that?

The book, I don't know where the book is. I need to read the next chapter. I need to commit to change.

I am more than tired. I am willing to end it.
